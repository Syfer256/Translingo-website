How It Works

For Employers

  1. Post Your Job - Describe your translation needs and budget
  2. Review Proposals - Receive proposals from qualified translators
  3. Choose & Pay - Select the best translator and funds go into escrow
  4. Receive Work - Get your translation and approve the work
  5. Leave Review - Rate your experience and help others

For Translators

  1. Create Profile - Showcase your languages and expertise
  2. Browse Jobs - Find projects matching your skills
  3. Submit Proposal - Pitch your services with a cover letter
  4. Deliver Work - Complete the translation on time
  5. Get Paid - Receive payment securely after approval
